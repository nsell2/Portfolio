About Me

Hi there! My name is Noah Sell and I am a senior at Arizona State University studying Full-Stack Web Development. I plan on graduating in the summer of 2025, and while I'm currently based out of Fargo, North Dakota, I hope to move to the Minneapolis/St. Paul area by next fall.

After taking my first online bootcamp HTML coding course, I was sucked into the world of web development and knew that I had to learn more. I've had a lot of fun over the last few years learning about different coding languages and using them to create various projects. Every project I complete feels like a puzzle I get to solve, and when I'm finished it's incredibly satisfying and rewarding to step back and see the final result.
